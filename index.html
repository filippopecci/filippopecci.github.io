Filippo Pecci

Researcher in Optimization & Infrastructure Systems

Imperial College London

I am a Research Associate in the Department of Civil and Environmental Engineering at Imperial College London. I received a PhD in computational optimization from Imperial College London in 2018. Before joining Imperial College, I completed BSc and MSc degrees in Mathematics at Università degli Studi di Padova, Italy.

My research focuses on building computational tools for mathematical optimization with applications to infrastructure systems.
